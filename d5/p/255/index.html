I replied that there was a time when I was also excessively worried about tests but now not anymore, and showed her D5P101-171207.

⑤ 495 asked me tons and tons of questions; although some of the questions are a bit silly and slightly annoying, most of her incessant inquiries are insightful, and they greatly push me to understand the concepts more deeply as well. When I don’t know something, she’ll ask 474 instead, so as I begin to like her, I feel motivated to work hard so that she asks me more…

⑥ One drawback of 495’s inquiries is that she sometimes asks me about the solution when I haven’t looked at the solution yet. I, on the other hand, tend to let her infer what my questions are before asking them.

⑧ On 10/31, 495 received the job offer from GS. My mind was mostly blank from 17:00 to 18:00 (that hour felt so long to me). I thought about how I lost both a competitor and a great companion in my quest for grad school (actually, the only companion, since I’m not familiar with most other seniors), and about how our friendship might dissolve as she begins to enjoy life following her offer.

From my experience in early-mid September, I agree that having nothing to do is far more painful than leading a busy life.

⑩ Although the starting salary in the finance industry is similar to tech ($11k), growth is exponential once you get promoted. The downside is that the work is really tiring. As Philip Guo said, the banking industry doesn’t have much tolerance for mistakes, making the work highly intense.
