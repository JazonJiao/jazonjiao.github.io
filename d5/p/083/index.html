① In the afternoon in our Visions group meeting, we built marshmallow towers in groups of five, and although I joined with another civil engineer Matt, our tower ultimately failed.

⑤ I tried to read and let concepts click with my mind, leaving a vague “neural path” that will suffice for multiple choice questions, because rigid reciting is definitely ineffective not only for memorizing, but also for long-term benefits in mindset.

⑦ 979 later talked to me about how she exploded in the exam; she’s contacted me 7 times but I’ve done none from my side, but I have an intuition that we’re not necessarily the best fit, so I’ll just treat her as a friend. My friendship with 671 is also developing quickly; in my conversations with her I never struggle to come up with topics because she does most of the job.

⑧ The score of the CS test also came out today; I scored a very unexpected 94, 133 got 88, 139 got 84, class average below 70. The score was placed in spam so I initially didn’t find it; on discovering the spam section I found that BOA sent a lot of emails to me…

⑨ After learning the score, I was astonished. By now except for the Chinese history test, on which I should have gotten good scores, mid-terms are complete, and my performance has exceeded my best anticipation.
