⑧ The Americans’ talk is extremely fast-paced and everyone is laughing wildly. I cannot engage in their conversations because (1) there is never even a second of silence when the guys are talking, and I don’t have a chance; (2) their topics are often unfamiliar to me; (3) I sometimes can’t understand them because they talk way too fast; (4) I definitely don’t have the English oral skills as natives.
