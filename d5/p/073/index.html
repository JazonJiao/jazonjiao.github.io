② I then think about the general trend of studying inertia consumption. When we first enter school, we tend to be conscientious and are afraid that we do anything wrong, but over time, as we find out that the rules are not strictly enforced, we lose momentum.

③ That’s why I’m certain that I’d study attentively in the first at least two months but am unsure about my performance from then on. There might be so many factors that make me lose the inertia and care less about my grades.
